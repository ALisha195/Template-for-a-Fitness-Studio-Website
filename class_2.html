Fitness Guide

Cardiovascular Exercise

Cardiovascular exercise, often referred to as cardio or aerobic exercise, is crucial for improving cardiovascular health and burning calories. Some common cardio exercises include:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Jumping rope
  • Brisk walking

Cardio workouts help strengthen your heart, improve lung capacity, and boost endurance.



Strength Training

Strength training, also known as resistance training, focuses on building muscle strength and tone. Key elements of strength training include:

  • Weight lifting with dumbbells or barbells
  • Bodyweight exercises like push-ups and squats
  • Resistance band workouts
  • Functional strength exercises
  • Core and stability training

Strength training helps increase muscle mass, boost metabolism, and enhance overall strength and balance.
